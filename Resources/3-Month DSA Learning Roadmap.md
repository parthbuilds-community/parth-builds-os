# 3-Month DSA Learning Roadmap

## Month 1: Foundations

### Week 1-2: Arrays & Strings
- Two pointers technique
- Sliding window pattern
- Common problems: reverse, palindrome, anagrams
- **Goal:** 15-20 easy problems

### Week 3-4: Hash Maps & Sets
- Frequency counting
- Two sum variations
- Finding duplicates
- **Goal:** 15 easy, 5 medium problems

## Month 2: Intermediate Concepts

### Week 5-6: Recursion
- Base case + recursive case pattern
- Practice: factorial, Fibonacci, power function
- Understand call stack visualization
- **Goal:** 10 recursive problems

### Week 7-8: Backtracking
- Permutations and combinations
- Subset generation
- Constraint satisfaction
- **Goal:** 5-8 medium problems (these are hard)

## Month 3: Advanced Structures

### Week 9-10: Trees
- Binary trees: traversals (in/pre/post-order)
- Binary search trees: search, insert
- BFS and DFS on trees
- **Goal:** 12-15 problems

### Week 11-12: Graphs
- Representations: adjacency list/matrix
- BFS and DFS on graphs
- Connected components
- **Goal:** 10 medium problems

## Daily Practice Strategy
- Solve 1-2 problems every day
- Spend 30 minutes before looking at solution
- Review solutions even if you got it right
- Redo problems after 1 week

## Resources
- **Platform:** LeetCode (sort by acceptance rate)
- **Roadmap:** NeetCode 150
- **Visual learning:** YouTube - Abdul Bari, Back to Back SWE
- **Track progress:** Spreadsheet with date solved

## Interview Prep (Final 2 Weeks)
- Do 1 mock interview every other day
- Time yourself (45 minutes per problem)
- Practice explaining your approach out loud
- Review Big O for every solution
