# 📘 FIRST SEMESTER SURVIVAL KIT

> *The 5 things no one tells you before college starts — but every senior wishes they knew.*

---

## 🎯 The 5 Things You MUST Do in Your First Semester

### 1️⃣ Build Your LinkedIn Profile — TODAY
- Placements feel far away, but opportunities come *only* when your profile is ready.
- Add a professional photo, a decent headline, and at least one project/internship (even a small one).
- **Connect with seniors & professors** from Week 1.

### 2️⃣ Check Your College Email EVERY DAY
- Internships, hackathons, fests, research opportunities — 90% of students miss them simply because they don't check their official email.
- Make it a morning habit. No excuses.

### 3️⃣ Learn to Say "HAAN" in First Semester
- Event? **Haan.**
- Competition? **Haan.**
- Volunteer work? **Haan.**
- Reason? The more you explore in Year 1, the faster you'll figure out what you *actually* want to do.

### 4️⃣ Start Talking to Seniors — Right Now
- Notes, guidance, referral opportunities, insider tips.
- Sometimes, a 5-minute chat with a senior is worth more than 5 hours on Google.
- **Pro tip:** Ask specific questions, not "college kaisa hai."

### 5️⃣ Build ONE Side Project — No Perfect Idea Needed
- Doesn't have to be groundbreaking.
- A small, ugly, working project will teach you more than 100 lectures.
- Just start. Seriously.

## 🔁 BONUS: Habits That Separate Top 1% from Rest

| Do This ✅ | Avoid This ❌ |
|------------|----------------|
| Show up to class 5 mins early | Skip classes because "attendance nahi hai" |
| Sit in front row sometimes | Sit at the back with your phone |
| Ask at least 1 doubt per week | Stay quiet even when confused |
| Save professor contacts | Wait till exams to talk to teachers |

---

## 📌 Quick Resource Checklist for First Year

- [ ] LinkedIn profile created & optimized
- [ ] College email ID added to phone
- [ ] At least 3 seniors added on LinkedIn
- [ ] One society/volunteer role applied for
- [ ] One small side project started (even 1 page)
