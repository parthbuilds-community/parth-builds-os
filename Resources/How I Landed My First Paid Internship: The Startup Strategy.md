# How I Landed My First Paid Internship: The Startup Strategy

Hey! Thanks for your interest. Here's the exact step-by-step process I used to land my first paid internship by targeting startups directly.

### My Main Target: STARTUPS

## 🚀 Step 1: Finding the Right Contacts (Most Important Step)

**Platforms I Used:** LinkedIn & Gmail

### My Strategy:
1. **Monitor Hiring Posts**: Whenever I saw a startup posting about hiring, I'd immediately engage
2. **Direct Founder Outreach**: I searched for CEOs and Founders of promising startups
3. **Why This Works**: Founders make quick decisions and appreciate initiative

### How to Find Them:
- Search "[Startup Name] CEO" or "[Startup Name] Founder" on LinkedIn
- Look for people with "Founder", "CEO", or "Co-founder" in their title
- Check the "About" section of company pages for leadership info

### Key Elements Your Message Needs:
- **Specific compliment** about their startup
- **Clear value proposition** - what you can do for THEM
- **Proof of your skills** (mention projects or experience)
- **Professional but enthusiastic** tone

**📎 CRITICAL: Attach your resume to every message**
